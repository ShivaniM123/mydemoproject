Getting started

How do I sign up for the Spark Driver™ platform?

Visit SIGN UP on the Spark Driver website or download the Spark Driver app to begin enrollment. Select your zone, complete the enrollment form, and receive a confirmation email with enrollment status tracking.

When can I sign in to the Spark Driver app?

You can download the Spark Driver app at any time. You will be able to sign in once you have been approved from the waitlist.

Do I need to complete a background check?

Background screening may be conducted, including motor vehicle records and criminal background checks, in accordance with applicable laws.

How long does the background check take?

Background check results are typically available within 1-7 business days depending on state and county processes. You will receive email updates throughout the process.

How do I check the status of my application?

Sign in to your Spark Driver app to check your status. View "Enrollment status" under the Enrollment Checklist on the homepage.

What does it mean if the zone I want to join is full?

This means we are temporarily not adding new drivers to that geographic area. You will receive an SMS notification when openings occur.

How long does it take to get approved for the Spark Driver platform?

The timeline is unpredictable and you will receive ongoing communication. You must sign your contract and agree to the terms before approval.

How do I sign in to the Spark Driver app?

Use the email address you provided during enrollment to sign in on your mobile device.

What are My Metrics?

My Metrics is your personal performance reference including: on-time arrival, completion rate, acceptance rate, and customer rating. These metrics don't represent all Service Level Standards.

Earnings & rewards

What type of earnings are available on the Spark Driver app?

Trip earnings, tips, and incentives per completed offer. You keep 100% of customer confirmed tips.

When will I receive earnings with Spark Driver?

You can elect to receive your earnings through instant deposit or weekly disbursement via online banking or direct deposit. Earnings methods are offered by third-party providers.

What types of incentives are available?

Lump sum, tiered, earn-per-trip, and multi-trip earnings incentives with varying earning structures.

How will I know if I qualify for the Rewards Program?

You will receive an email the first week of the month with a unique Walmart+ membership code if you qualified.

What happens if I qualify for the Rewards Program one month but not the next?

Qualifications update monthly. You will have the opportunity to become eligible again the following month.

Will I get a new Walmart+ membership code every month for the Rewards Program?

Yes, a new code is provided each qualifying month.

What if I'm already a member of Walmart+?

Your membership extends based on the number of qualifying months. Membership is subject to Walmart+ Terms of Use.

Eligibility

Appealing a deactivation?

Please reference the provided documentation for appeal information.

What are the Spark Driver platform qualifications?

You must be age 18 or older, provide accurate personal information, be authorized to work in the U.S., have a valid REAL ID or driver's license, verify your identity via selfie, have a U.S. phone number, provide proof of auto insurance, have an active bank account, own a clean and reliable vehicle, and have a mobile device with camera and GPS.

Do I need to use a certain type of vehicle?

Any safe, reliable vehicle is acceptable. Motorcycles, motorized bicycles, and scooters are not adequate for deliveries.

Do I need to have auto insurance?

Yes, proof of auto insurance is required with your name, expiration date, and vehicle information. Details must be clear and unobstructed.

What kind of phone do I need to access the Spark Driver app?

You need a mobile device (iOS or Android 11+) with camera, flash, and GPS capabilities.

Can I also drive for other delivery apps while I'm signed up to deliver on the Spark Driver platform?

Yes, as an independent contractor, you may drive for any delivery provider.

General

How will I receive offers on the Spark Driver app?

Turn on "Spark Now" to receive offers. Each offer shows delivery type, timeframe, and estimated earnings. Acceptance is entirely optional.

How will I receive information and updates from the Spark Driver platform?

Through in-app notifications, text messages (SMS 32093), and emails regarding incentives, offers, and updates.

Do I get to choose the locations I want to deliver from?

Yes, you can select your geographic zone during enrollment and change it anytime via the app (Zone > Change).

Will I be delivering to residential customers or businesses?

Both options are available. You can choose residential, businesses, or both.

What are the delivery hours for the Spark Driver platform?

Delivery hours are 6:00 AM to 11:00 PM.

Can I have a passenger during Spark Driver deliveries?

Yes, but passengers cannot participate in Shopping & Delivery. Children too young to be left alone are prohibited.

What type of products will I deliver using the Spark Driver app?

Grocery, Dotcom, Walmart GoLocal, Pharmacy, Returns, and Shopping & Delivery orders. No single item exceeds 60 lbs.
