Types of offers

Delivery

You can pick up and deliver grocery and general merchandise orders for Walmart customers and our retail partners.

Shopping

Spark Driver—Shopping & Delivery offers involve driving to a specific store, shopping a list of items, and delivering the order.

Returns

You can return an order by picking it up from the customer and driving it back to a Walmart store.

Additional products for delivery

The products found in an order will vary depending on which offer you accept.

  • Dotcom — Dotcom offers involve delivering general merchandise orders from walmart.com, to customers.
  • Walmart GoLocal — Walmart GoLocal offers involve picking up orders from other retailers that sell hardware, car parts, or flowers.
  • Pharmacy — Pharmacy offers involve picking up and delivering prescriptions from a Walmart or Sam's Club.
